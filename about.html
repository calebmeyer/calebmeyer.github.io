About us

Helping Hands is a Food Pantry, run by volunteers. Our goal is to provide those in need, in the Odessa R-7 School district, with basic necessities, such as food, personal hygiene, cleaning supplies, paper goods, etc. We also have items like meat, butter, eggs, and cheese, along with pet food so your furry friends won't have to suffer. We want everyone who walks through the doors to have at least some portion of their needs met and have them be able to leave with their dignity intact.
